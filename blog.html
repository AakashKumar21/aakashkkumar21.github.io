Chat over Coffee
Thomson Smith - April 18,2016
Focal Point:the point at which rays or waves meet after reflection or refraction, or the point from which diverging rays or waves appear to proceed; the center of interest or activity. The dictionary definition of a “focal point” says a lot about what kind of program Passion Movement International has formed - broadly, a center of interest and activity. In fact, Delhi itself is a kind of epicenter of minds, a meeting place of thinkers. Some are striving for education, others for work, but all have an ambition towards a better future. With so many peoples of varied cultures, the Delhi melting point is boiling over with philosophies and beliefs.
